Chris Petitti’s Fight TactiX uses practical applications which can be easily acquired regardless of size and strength. Our training replicates real-life scenarios and environments which teaches students not only to protect themselves against an attacker but helps them physically and mentally prepare for the unexpected. Our training comes directly from Israel, and teaches Krav Maga in the fighting system's most modern evolution. Israeli Krav Maga has been tested and proven to be the most effective defense system on the planet.

From a young age Chris was passionate about sports and athletic player development. Raised in Newtown CT, he was a multi sport athlete. While attending Sacred Heart University he became fascinated with the security and safety field. He continued his education and began extensive training in executive protection and advanced security operations.

In 2002 he began providing security coverage for many well-known celebrities, as well as corporate executives and former government officials. Chris has had the great opportunity to extensively travel and has provided his services to clients all over the world.

Adding to his credentials, Chris is certified through the Israeli Krav Maga Association (Gidon System) as well as Nir Maman’s CT707 Israeli Special Forces Krav Maga. In 2012 he opened his first school to begin teaching Israeli Krav Maga to men and women in CT.
